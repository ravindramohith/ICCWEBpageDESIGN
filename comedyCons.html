comedy cons

description

Comedy Cons is the Stand Up Comedy Club of IIT Bombay. We provide a platform for beginners and enthusiasts to excel in the field of comedy by organizing regular jamming sessions, open mics, workshops and collaborative events. Targeted exposure and networking with professional comedians is our ultimate goal.
