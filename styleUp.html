StyleUp

description

StyleUp is the fashion club of IIT Bombay, but it is so much more than that. Its about giving people a platform to express themselves, a stage to boost their confidence and bring out the best version of themselves. Its for those aspiring fashion designers, pushing themselves towards that first step that everybody needs. Its about acceptance and love, everyone is beautiful. And above all its about its people, coming from different backgrounds and bonding over their love of style and became a family. We are StyleUp and StyleUp is us!
